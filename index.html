Featured Program

Refreshment Day

Monthly

At the end of every month, OSIS organizes simple games, sports, music, and activities during break, lunch, or after school.

Students get to showcase their talents in front of their friends.
Winners take home snacks as a small, fun reward.
The goal: better mental health and stronger friendships, one break at a time.
